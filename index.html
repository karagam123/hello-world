What is Jumbo Park?

Welcome to Jumbo Park, an interactive website designed to help Tufts students advertise and rent off-campus parking spaces throughout the semester. Whether you're heading out for a long weekend, looking to rent out a parking spot for a full school year, or just trying to find a place to keep your car, Jumbo Park can help. Create an account and get started today!
